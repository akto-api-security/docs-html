Quick start with Akto Self Hosted

Akto can be self-hosted in just 60 seconds.

Introduction

If you're like us, you probably have a lot of data that is only accessible from your private network (locally or behind your VPN). That's why we built the self-hosted version of Akto.

How to deploy?

Deploy Akto locally or on your own cloud. Choose any of the below options to get started:
  1. 1.
    ​AWS deploy​
  2. 2.
    ​GCP Deploy​
  3. 3.
    ​Local Deploy​
